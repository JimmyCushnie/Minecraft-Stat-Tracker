Currently, Minecraft objectives have a maximum character length of 16. There
are over 5,000 objectives in this list. Writing a useful name under 16 characters
for each of them would take forever to do by hand, and would be very difficult
to do with code. Therefore, I have elected to name each objective with a number
instead of text. Use this document to look up the number of a particular statistic.

On the left is how the objective shows up in-game, and on the right is the number
you use to refer to it using commands (i.e. scoreboard objectives setdisplay)
